About Faezeh Rohani, PhD

This section is prepared as editable placeholder copy, based on the structure you provided.

I am an academic researcher focused on artificial intelligence in medicine, health data science, and clinically meaningful innovation. My work centers on developing trustworthy AI methods that can support diagnosis, prognosis, clinical validation, and better decision-making across multiple healthcare data sources.

I collaborate with researchers, clinicians, students, and software developers to turn computational methods into usable research systems. The lab welcomes interdisciplinary work across explainable AI, medical imaging, biomedical signal processing, mental health informatics, and education in AI-powered healthcare.
